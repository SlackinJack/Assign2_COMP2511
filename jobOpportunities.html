Taking Computer Science as your program of choice presents various job opportunities in the real world. For example, one can choose to become a:

  • Web Developer
  • Software Developer
  • IT

  • Computer Security Researcher

Many international corporations and businesses hire Computer Science graduates. Below are a few "big names" to send your application to:

  • Red Hat
  • CrowdStrike
  • Zimperium
  • White Ops
  • Avast
