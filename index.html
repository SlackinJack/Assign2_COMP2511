What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers focusing on software and hardware. There are multiple areas of study within the computer science major such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Network Infratucture
  • Computer Security
  • Programming Languages

The key purpose of the degree to identify problems and create algorithms and study the performance of computer hardware and software.

The computer science major is also a very well paying field of study (figure 1.1)

Why study Computer Science

Computer Science is a field of study the opportunities are endless in the sense that since technology is improving rapidly the need for people who can understand the technology. Computer Science also provides the skill of problem solving which is a very important life skill. If we look around us there is technology everywhere, ranging from a smart speaker to a supercomputer at NASA. A very important aspect of computer science is that we take old devices and improve them to work up to our standards, for example:
In 1956 IBM developed a hard-drive that cost $10,100 and it had a maximum capacity of 5MB. As a result of techonological innovation Seagate has delveloped a $600 hard drive with a capacity of 16TB!
